A Brief History of Navette

Founding the Company

Navette is founded in Norway. The company is born out of an idea to design and manufacture pleasure boats. Initially there are 3 founders, but later Rune Nilsen buys all shares.

Year 2001
Launch of S 565

After a year of hard work and effort, the first boat - Navette S 565 is launched into production.

Year 2002
Cutting Prices

Production is outsourced to a Latvian company with a goal to cut costs while maintaining the quality. This would result in lowered prices and therefore product being more available to the customer.

Year 2003
Ensuring the Quality

To ensure quality control Navette expands its operations to Latvia - estabilishing a production facility in Saldus. The facility is small but for now - satisfactory.

Year 2003
Launch of S 540

The second boat in the series - Navette S 540 is introduced to the market.

Year 2005
A Change of Mindset

Due to pleasure boat market being very unstable, a decision is made to also produce other products. At the same time Navette also expands to much larger and its own production facilities near Saldus.

Year 2005
Fiberglass boddies

Navette produces fiberglass bodies for a city electric car Buddy, the sixth generation of the Kewet electric vehicle.

Year 2007-2010
Hard to top

In cooperation with Loyds Industri begins fiberglass hardtop production for Toyota Hilux. Not too long after, Navette designs and launches its own hardtop for VW Amarok.
