S 540

Description

S 540 is a sporty console boat with great performance. A special emphasis was put on ergonomics during the development stage of the boat. Seats are comfortable and it is possible to move around in the boat with ease. The cockpit is among the absolute best. The driver has a good overview of instruments and water no matter if standing or sitting. Steering wheel and gas/trim sits right where they should be and there is plenty of room for your legs under the steering consle. The windshield serves its purpose and provides good protection behind the control console. The hull has a relatively deep V-bottom which makes the ride soft even in rough waters. Det er håndopplagt med triaksial duk i skroget. Hekken er spesielt kraftig da motorfundamentet er trukket noe bak hekken og forsterket direkte i langsgående stringere. Båten lander mykt i sjøen, hekken slipper ikke og det er bortimot umulig å kavitere propellen. Under the console there is spacious storage room, while under the stern bench seat is located battery, tankinspeksjon and a large storage locker.

Quality - Security

The boat has an adequate self-bailing system, which is one of the most important safety features of any boat. High quality gelcoat and GRP materials are used in production, ensuring great physical properties and resistance to water. Therefore you dont have to worry about things like osmosis. The composite layer of both hull and superstructure includes stitched triaxal fiberglass mat, which allows for excellent strength-to-weight ratio. All on-board equipment is of high quality. All fittings and accessories are made from corrosion and acid resistant materials.
